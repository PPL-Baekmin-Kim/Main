Atmospheric Circulation and Practice

Course Objectives

1. Learn how to write and use adiabatic diagram and meteorological message.
2. Learn how to write and use surface weather chart, upper air weather chart and supplementary weather chart.
3. Learn the ability to analyze cases of actual severe weather such as heavy rainfall and typhoons by using adiabatic diagram and surface weather chart.
